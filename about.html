WadFitness

WadFitness is all about strength, community, and growth. Whether you're just starting your fitness journey or you're an experienced lifter. We’ve built this space with a passion for fitness, and we’re excited to share it with you.

About Me

My name is Wada Mehari, the host of the Push-Pull competition. I am a state and national record holder for the 'Women's Raw Drug Tested Juniors (20-23) 60kg class'. As an international student from Ethiopia, I discovered the power of strength training through my close friend and mentor, Joya Drenth. Inspired by her strength and dedication, I began my own fitness journey, and now, I’m passionate about creating a community where others can also find empowerment through fitness.

Our Partners

Gast Gym

Gast Gym is a 24-hour fitness facility located in Addis Ababa. They believe in making fitness accessible to everyone, no matter their lifestyle. Gast Gym offers world-class equipment in a welcoming, no-frills environment where everyone can focus on their fitness goals. Their commitment to providing top-notch facilities at an affordable price makes them an ideal partner for WadFitness.

Instagram: @gastgym

Natty Twins

Natty Twins x Vibes is a powerhouse collaboration between renowned bodybuilding influencers Natty Twins, Markos, and Yohannes, and the thriving gym wear brand Vibes. This partnership merges the Natty Twins’ dynamic presence and influence in the fitness community with Vibes’ reputation for high-quality, performance-driven apparel. Together, they aim to revolutionize gym wear by offering a line that embodies both style and functionality, catering to fitness enthusiasts who seek to push their limits while looking their best. This bold venture promises to dominate the fitness apparel market, blending authenticity, innovation, and the powerful reach of fitness icons with a brand known for its trendsetting designs.

Instagram: @natty.twins1

Ethio Fitness Nutrition (EFN)

EFN is a trusted name in the Ethiopian fitness community. With locations in Addis Ababa, Adama, Bahir Dar, and Hossana, EFN offers high-quality supplements and expert nutritional advice to help you reach your fitness goals. Their commitment to providing products that support healthy lifestyles makes them an ideal partner for WadFitness, where we focus on not just lifting, but on holistic well-being.

Instagram: @ethio_fitness_nutrition
